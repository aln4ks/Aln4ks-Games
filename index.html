What is Aln4ks Games?+
Aln4ks Games is a company that creates popular party games like YOU DON’T KNOW ALN4KS, Fish, Bonch, Sims, Bad Murder Party, and more! We describe ourselves as a quirky mix of classic social games like ‘charades’ but with a unique twist, kind of like the rebellious child who won over the family by starting a successful jam company.
What is a Party Pack?+
We create a wide range of games suitable for gatherings, online meetings, or live streams on platforms like Zoom and Twitch. Our collection includes drawing games, writing games, trivia games, and hidden identity games, ensuring that there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Since 2014, we have been releasing a new set of games annually, known as a Jackbox Party Pack. Each pack contains 5 different games designed for 1-8 players. Even if you have a larger group, many of our games support audience play-along, allowing even more people to join in on the fun!

In addition to the Party Packs, we also offer standalone titles such as Drawful 2, which provide unique gaming experiences outside of the main collections.
Got it! Your friends and family just need to be ready to have fun!+
All your players need is a smartphone or web-enabled device! There's no need for an app or extra purchases in order for them to play. They just need a mobile device, internet access, and the ability to see the screen running the game.
How do I get a Jackbox Game Started?+
You've got a Party Pack and your crew assembled. Now all you need to do is start the game and play! For guidance on connecting all your players to the game, we provide a helpful video tutorial and step-by-step instructions on this page. You'll also find information about accessing additional features and customization options for each game.

The key is to ensure that everyone in your group, whether together or remote, can see and hear the game screen. This way, they can view the room code, join the game, and follow the action to play along.
What should I do if I experience issues while playing a Aln4ks Game?+
We have a comprehensive support site with plenty of helpful tips and troubleshooting methods available. Additionally, you can submit a support ticket, and our dedicated team strives to respond within 1-3 business days. During holidays, it might take a bit longer due to a higher volume of inquiries.

One of our top tips is that if a player's mobile device disconnects during the game, they should try refreshing their web browser. In many cases, this action instantly reconnects them. If this doesn't work, they should reach out to us for assistance. Remember, we're committed to ensuring that no player is left behind!
